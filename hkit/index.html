Our Story

A legacy of purposeful architecture, rooted in place

In continuous practice in the Bay Area since 1948, HKIT Architects brings enthusiasm, commitment, and invaluable experience to every project. We maintain an average of 60 professional staff dedicated to design and client relationships.

Our size and structure allow for a level of specialization and client attentiveness characteristic of small firms, while affording the strength and flexibility of a larger group of professionals. Every project is a partnership.

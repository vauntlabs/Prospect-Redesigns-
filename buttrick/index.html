The Practice

Architecture as an open tool to problem solve and uncover spaces

Led by Jerome Buttrick since inception, Buttrick Projects Architecture + Design centers on using the creative process to solve complex residential puzzles. Rather than enforcing a rigid default style, our solutions unfold from the precise program, topography, and light paths of the site.

We typically guide assignments from conceptual sketch and public approvals through structural specifications to finish selections and lighting design. We enjoy uncovering unexpected ways of seeing to bring lasting clarity to custom environments.
